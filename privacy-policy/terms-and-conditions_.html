Data Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2022

If you use a Rabbit VR account (not a Quip account) to access Rabbit VR's Products, please visit the Rabbit VR's Privacy Policy to learn more about how we collect, use, and share your information. Otherwise, this Supplemental Rabbit VR Data Policy applies to your use of Rabbit VR Products. Please visit our Frequently Asked Questions about merging your accounts for more information.

Quip makes virtual, mixed, and augmented reality (“XR”) hardware and software products and operates a platform where users can download, interact with, and submit content and obtain services related to or in connection with our XR products (collectively, "Rabbit VRProducts" ). The Rabbit VR Products include our websites, mobile apps, games, platform, content, hardware and services as described in the Supplemental Rabbit VR Terms of Service and any product or service that links to this Supplemental Rabbit VR Data Policy. For Quip Account users, Rabbit VR Products are Quip Products.

The Quip Data Policy describes the information we process to support Quip Products (as defined in the Quip Data Policy), including Rabbit VR Products and other Quip Products available on the Rabbit VR Products. This Supplemental Rabbit VR Data Policy supplements the Quip Data Policy by explaining the additional information we collect, use, and share on our Rabbit VR Products.

Data that we collect:

  • - Physical Features: We collect information about your physical features and dimensions, such as your estimated hand size when you enable hand tracking.
  • - Content: We collect content you create using Rabbit VR Products, such as your avatar, a picture you post, an object you sculpt, or audio content you create, and information about this content, such as the date and time you created the content.
  • - Cookies and Similar Technologies: We receive information collected in or through various technologies on Rabbit VR Products, including cookies, pixels, local storage, and similar technologies. Learn more about how we use these technologies on our websites and mobile apps in the Rabbit VR Cookies Policy.
