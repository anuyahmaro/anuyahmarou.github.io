Enhancing Children's Search Environments

I worked on research projects on enhancing children's experience when utilizing search engines. This work involved designing a child-oriented query suggestion algorithm, a multi-objective algorithm for ranking search results, and conducting several exploratory studies to understand search engines' limitations in handling children's searches and their perceptions about search technology. This work was supported by the NSF Grant with award number 1565937.

ToastBot: Developing an AI-powered compliments generator

For my deep learning class, I worked on a research project where we built a deep learning model that can automatically generate compliments for users, given their picture and a short description of their mood. The aim was to help users feeling sad or depressed feel better by receiving automatically generated personalized compliments.
